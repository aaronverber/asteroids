Play a game largely similar to Asteroids!

by Aaron Verber

Click PLAY to begin. Use the following controls to score as many points as you can. If you are hit 10 times, you lose.

(I'm still working out some of the kinks. It seems to play best so far in Chrome for Windows.)

Up
Accelerate Forward
Down
Accelerate Backward
Left
Turn left
Right
Turn right
Shift
Apply (gentle) break
Spacebar
Fire weapon
Ctrl
Raise temporary shields (three uses)
